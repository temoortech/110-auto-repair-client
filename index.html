About Us

London's Most Trusted Garage

Founded in 2011, 110 Auto Repair Shop has been serving drivers across London with expert, honest and affordable vehicle repair and servicing. Based at 110 High Street, we handle everything from routine oil changes to complex engine overhauls.

Our team of IMI-certified mechanics work with all makes and models — from hatchbacks to SUVs — using the latest diagnostic equipment and quality parts. We believe every customer deserves straightforward advice and fair pricing.

Our Mission

To provide every London driver with dealership-quality repairs at honest, independent garage prices — building long-term relationships through transparency, skill and integrity.
