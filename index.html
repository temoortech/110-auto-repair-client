About Us

Manchester's Most Trusted Garage

Shah Jee Auto Garage is your local mechanic and auto-electrician in Longsight, Manchester. Based at Unit B, Nutsford Vale (Off Pink Bank Lane), we handle everything from engine repairs and clutch replacements to electronic diagnostics and full electrical fault-finding.

Our experienced team works with all makes and models — European, Japanese, Korean and American vehicles — using the latest diagnostic equipment and quality parts. We believe every customer deserves straightforward advice and honest pricing, every time.

Our Mission

To provide every Manchester driver with reliable, expert repairs at fair, independent garage prices — building long-term relationships through transparency, skill and integrity.
